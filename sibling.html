What is SIBLING?

Sharing Information By Leading, Inspiring, and Nurturing Growth (SIBLING) is a mentorship program that pairs an upperclassman mentor with an underclassman mentee. SIBLING seeks to create a less intimidating environment for PILLS members, a sense of camaraderie with fellow pre-pharmacy students, and to harbor lasting relationships between upperclassman and underclassmen peers. We will do our best to pair you up with a mentor or mentee with similar background and interests.

If you have any questions, please email the SIBLING coordinators, Christabel Lo (c.lo@berkeley.edu) and Rock Feng (rfeng@berkeley.edu).
