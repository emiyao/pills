You must attend at least one social per semester to be considered an active member. We strongly encourage you to attend as many as you can and get to meet other PILLS members!
Contact Public Relations Chair Ann Chen (kac1145@berkeley.edu) if you have any questions regarding the socials.

 

8th Social: Scavenger Hunt

April 7 (6:00PM-7:30pm) Memorial Glade
Come participate in a fun scavenger hunt with fellow PILLS members around the UC Berkeley campus! We will be competing in teams to finish the tasks and the winners will receive a very special prize. Hope to see you there!
Contact Kathy Chong (kchong123@berkeley.edu) or Christabel Lo (c.lo@berkeley.edu) if you have any questions regarding the social.
-- PILLS Public Relations Committee 2015-2016

 

7th Social: InterHealth Social

March 4 (4:00PM-5:30PM) Memorial Glade
Enjoying this sunny weather? PILLS and ForeSight will be having a picnic potluck social on Memorial Glade! Bring food and games and come out for some fun in the sun!
Contact Tiffany Kwok (tiffanypwkwok@berkeley.edu) or Tran Truong (trantruong@berkeley.edu) if you have any questions regarding the social.
-- PILLS Public Relations Committee 2015-2016

 

6th Social: Fashion Show

February 16 (6:00PM-7:00PM) 151 Barrows
Come on out to the first PILLS social of the semester! We will be splitting off into teams to create outfits for our annual PILLS fashion show. Come and let your inner diva out!
Contact Mehr Virk (mvirk@berkeley.edu) or Minjong Kim (mjkim9511@berkeley.edu) if you have any questions regarding the social.
-- PILLS Public Relations Committee 2015-2016

 

5th Social: Gingerbread Houses

December 4 (7:00PM-8:00PM) 79 Dwinelle
Come out to our 5th and FINAL social of the semester! We will be listening to holiday music and splitting up into teams to make gingerbread houses. We will provide building and decorating materials, but feel free to bring your own sweet treats! As a reminder, you need to attend at least 1 social per semester to become an active member. Hope to see you all there!
Contact Mehr Virk (mvirk@berkeley.edu), Tiffany Kwok (tiffanypkwok@berkeley.edu), or Kathy Chong (kchong123@berkeley.edu) if you have any questions regarding the social.
-- PILLS Public Relations Committee 2015-2016
