Benefits of Being a Member

-- Priority access to Pharmacy Q&A sessions with pharmacists and pharmacy students
-- Priority access to PILLS hosted workshops (e.g. pharmacy technician workshop, interview workshop)
-- Discount on NCPPS tickets (see the NCPPS tab for more information)
-- Eligibility to apply to NCPPS committee and PRC committee
-- Opportunity to shadow a pharmacist at the end of the academic year


How to Become an Active Member

-- Submit membership application online
-- Pay membership dues ($15/year; Early: $12/year)
-- Attend 2 General Meetings
-- Attend 1 PILLS-approved volunteering event
-- Attend 1 PILLS social
