8th General Meeting: Pharmacy School Prerequisites and Careers in Pharmacy

March 16 (7:00PM-8:30PM) 156 Dwinelle
Do you need help choosing your classes for the Fall semester? Are you unsure what pharmacy is all about? Come to our 8th general meeting, where we will be going over pharmacy school prerequisites and providing information on which UC Berkeley classes fulfill those requirements (reminder: Tele-Bears is coming up!). We will also introduce a variety of careers in pharmacy such as retail, ambulatory care, consultant, and more. We look forward to seeing you there!
-- PILLS Executive Board 2015-2016

5th General Meeting: Student Pharmacist Panel

November 18 (7:00PM-8:30PM) 166 Barrows
Come out to our 5th and FINAL general meeting of the semester. Student pharmacists from UCSF, Touro, and University of Pacific will come to speak about their experiences in pharmacy school, undergrad, and the application process. They will also be able to answer any questions you may have for them. As a reminder, you need to attend 2 general meetings to become an active member, so make sure you come out!
-- PILLS Executive Board 2015-2016

2nd General Meeting: Careers in Pharmacy & Pharmacy Guest Speaker

September 30 (7:00PM-8:30PM) 213 Wheeler
Unsure what pharmacy is all about? Come to our 2nd general meeting where we will be going over a variety of careers in pharmacy such as clinical, retail, ambulatory care, and more. A retail and hospital pharmacist will be present to speak on their firsthand experiences and advice pertaining to pursing pharmacy. There will be a Q&A session for the pharmacist, so feel free to come prepared with any questions related to pharmacy school and beyond.
Additionally, we will present a club leadership opportunity for our members by talking about how you can apply to become part of the 10th Annual NCPPS committee!
-- PILLS Executive Board 2015-2016
