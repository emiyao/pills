You must attend at least one volunteering event per semester to be considered an active member. We strongly encourage you to attend as many as you can and get to meet other PILLS members!
Contact External Relations Officer Eugenia Tang (eugeniat94@berkeley.edu) if you have any questions regarding the volunteering events.


*After signing up, wait for a confirmation email a few days before the event. You are required to reply to the confirmation email in order to attend. Meeting location for all events is in front of the Golden Bear Cafe (GBC)*

 

9th Volunteering Event: [Inter-health Project] Rebuilding Together

April 23 (9AM-12PM) 1717 Cedar Street
Come join PILLS and other Pre-Health organizations join Rebuilding Together with organizing Tim's house! Tim is a local here at Berkeley and a well-known hippie. He unfortunately is sick and disabled, which prevents him from working and so he wants to remodel his home such that he can rent it out. We will be helping him clean out the rooms and bookshelves! Both breakfast and lunch is provided as well as a free T-shirt! Don't forget to fill out BOTH sign-up forms.

8th Volunteering Event: Prisoners Literature Project

April 17 (2-5PM) and April 24 (2-5PM) 2022 Blake Street
Do you like books and want to brighten a day for someone who really needs it? Join PILLS in helping Prisoners Literature Project pick and send books to prisoners around the United States. These prisoners request these books themselves, so it truly means a lot when we are able to send it to them. Let's work together on helping such a great cause! Each visit is limited to 4 people max, so sign up fast!

Sign-up Form


 

7th Volunteering Event: Eggster

April 2 (12:45PM-4:00PM) Memorial Glade
Want to help a fellow student group run a free, fun event for children and their families? Come join PILLS in helping Eggster with their annual Egg Hunt and Learning Festival! We will be conducting egg hunts and supervising learning booths. Come on by!

 

6th Volunteering Event: Chinese Martial Arts Tournament

March 12 (8:45AM-12:00PM) RSF
"Started by Sifu Bryant Fong of Cal Wushu in 1992, Chinese Martial Arts Tournament is now the largest Chinese martial arts tournament to be held in the Western hemisphere. CMAT is an annual volunteer-run tournament featuring contemporary, traditional, internal, and sanshou events." -Cal Wushu Website
Come join PILLS in helping Cal Wushu in their biggest event of the year! We will be setting up mats as well as any other miscellaneous tasks they may need to make sure the event runs smoothly. We also get to watch the events, too!

 

5th Volunteering Event: J-Sei's 25th Annual Crab Feed

February 21 (5:00PM-8:00PM) meet at Underhill Parking Lot
Come join PILLS to help J-Sei serve guests at their annual Crab Feed! J-Sei is a non-profit organization dedicated to taking care of seniors, many of Japanese descent, in the Bay Area. Come help support this noble cause and have some fun with PILLS members. If we arrive by 5PM, we get to eat some crab for free, too!

 

4th Volunteering Event: Cerrito Creek

December 5 (10:00AM-12:30PM) Cerrito Creek
Want to give back to the community as well as nature? Come join PILLS and Friends of Five Creeks in restoring the natural wildlife of El Cerrito by planting native plants and getting rid of various litter! For more information on the event, visit www.fivecreeks.org/calendar.shtml!

 

3rd Volunteering Event: Berkeley Project

October 24 (8:00AM-4:00PM) Berkeley
Come join PILLS in the biggest citywide volunteering event in Berkeley! Let's all make Berkeley better with fellow PILLSters. For more information on the event, go to www.berkeleyproject.org! NOTE: After you sign up on our form, you need register and sign up with PILLS on the Berkeley Project website!

 

2nd Volunteering Event: Sunday Streets Berkeley

October 18 (9:00AM-11:00AM) Shattuck Avenue
Sunday Streets (also called "Open Streets) is an event where a portion of Shattuck Avenue gets closed down for a street festival. Come help set up for this wonderful event, and then maybe stick around for the event itself! For more information on the event, visit www.sundaystreetsberkeley.org!

 

Ongoing Volunteering Event: St. Mark's Hot Meals for the Hungry

Every 3rd Saturday of the month (First shift: 1:30PM-4:00PM; Second shift: 4:30PM-6:30PM) St. Mark's Church
PILLS will be assisting St. Mark's Church every month in their event, Hot Meals for the Hungry, where we prepare and serve meals for 120 hungry individuals living in Berkeley. If you enjoy creating delicious food and fond memories, come out to the event! Sign up fast, though, because spots are limited!
