Presented by the UC Berkeley Pre-Pharmacy Informational Learning and Leadership Society

Sunday, April 3rd, 2016 | 8:30am-4:30pm | Stanley Hall at UC Berkeley

About NCPPS

Every year, hundreds of students and speakers gather at UC Berkeley for the Northern California Pre-Pharmacy Symposium (NCPPS). The day long symposium will feature workshops on different topics in pharmacy, presentations by school representatives, pharmacist and pharmacy student panels, and information on pharmacy school applications.


Attend Workshops

Network

Get Answers

Have Fun


Whether you are just curious about the field of pharmacy and healthcare or a student planning to apply to pharmacy school, NCPPS has something for you! Network with professionals from around the Bay Area, interact with fellow students, and attend exciting workshops! NCPPS is your chance to explore what pharmacy is all about.

As NCPPS is a professional symposium that allows attendees to network with pharmacists, pharmacy school representatives, pharmacy students, and many others in the field of pharmacy, we suggest that attendees dress in business casual attire.

Registration

Tickets for NCPPS can be bought on Eventbrite starting Monday, February 1st, or in person at our PILLS table on Sproul between 10am-2pm. The price for active PILLS members is $13, and $16 for non-members. Get your ticket and secure your spot at this year's NCPPS!


Your ticket includes:
- Attendance for the keynote speech and welcoming ceremony
- Entry to 20+ workshops
- Networking luncheon with pharmacists, students, and school representatives
- Pharmacy school fair
- Breakfast, lunch, and refreshments
- Additional resources

Parking and Directions

This year, NCPPS will be held in Stanley Hall on the UC Berkeley campus.

Directions to NCPPS:
Berkeley Bart Station: Upon exiting the BART station, walk East on Center towards the bus stop located at Shattuck Ave:Center St. right outside the store Games of Berkeley. Take the F bus heading towards the UC Campus to the stop at GayleyRd:Greek Theater. Disembark and walk North up Gayley Rd. and turn left onto University Drive. Stanley Hall will be the first building to your right. The entrance is right next to a Yali's Cafe. You will see signs directing you to NCPPS Registration Table.

I-580/I-80 E: Take Exit 11 to merge onto University Ave. Turn left onto Oxford. Turn right onto Hearst Ave. Continue on Hearst Ave. past Scenic Ave. The Lower Hearst Parking Structure will be located to your left. Proceed to Level 2 to the Gold Zone in the east side of the lot for public parking. Walk outside of the lot and proceed to walk East on Hearst Ave. Turn right onto Gayley Rd. Walk until you reach University Dr. and turn right. Stanley Hall will be the first building to your right. Follow signs directing you to the registration table.
