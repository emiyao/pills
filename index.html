Welcome to PILLS at UC Berkeley!

The Pre-Pharmacy Informational Learning and Leadership Society (PILLS) is dedicated to serving students interested in pharmacy. As the primary pre-pharmacy organization at UC Berkeley, PILLS provides students with resources and support for pursuing pharmacy as a career. Our goal is to provide information about pharmacy and related fields, and to guide students through the transition from UC Berkeley to pharmacy school.

Why Join PILLS?

Throughout the school year, PILLS organizes informative general meetings (inviting pharmacy-related guest speakers), and provides numerous resources, such as the SIBLING and PhREND programs, to give you a better insight of the pharmacy field. By joining PILLS, you will not only receive these and countless other benefits, but also gain knowledge of the pharmacy field, find a community of like-minded students, and be prepared for applying to pharmacy school.

PILLS is not exclusively for pre-pharmacy students. Whether you are someone who is planning to go to pharmacy school or one who is still undecided about the field, feel free to drop by one of our events or contact any of the PILLS officers for more information.

10th Annual Northern California Pre-Pharmacy Symposium

Interested in pre-health or curious about a career in pre-pharmacy? NCPPS is the largest pre-pharmacy gathering of its kind in Northern California, attracting around 300 attendees each year. It includes workshops led by pharmacists of diverse specialties, panels of pharmacists and pharmacy students, as well as a networking luncheon and school fair. These features provide attendees with a forum for the exchange of information and resources.
