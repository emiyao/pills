What is PhREND?

Pre-Pharmacy Readiness, Enrichment, Networking, and Development (PhREND) is a program where you can meet other people who are interested in pharmacy in a smaller group setting. You can attend PhREND group socials to meet other people in the club and participate in fun activities with your PhREND group!

If you have any questions, please email the PhREND coordinators, Jay Pan (jay4662@berkeley.edu), Minjong Kim (mjkim9511@berkeley.edu), and Tran Truong (trantruong@berkeley.edu).
